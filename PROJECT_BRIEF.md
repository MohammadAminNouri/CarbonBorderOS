# Project Brief — CarbonBorderOS

## One-line pitch

CarbonBorderOS is a live CBAM intelligence platform that converts import ledgers into carbon-border cost, supplier risk, company exposure profiles, and material/procurement actions.

## The gap

The web already has many CBAM calculators. They usually stop at a static formula. The stronger product is a decision system that combines:

- regulatory rule engine
- live/manual carbon price signal
- import ledger analytics
- supplier data-quality checks
- estimated company exposure dictionary
- anomaly detection
- materials substitution logic
- Excel reporting

## Novelty

The new angle is not the CBAM formula. The novelty is the **operating system around CBAM**:

```text
import data → CBAM scope → emissions source → cost → risk → company dictionary → scenario → action
```

## Target users

- EU importers
- non-EU exporters selling into the EU
- procurement teams
- sustainability teams
- customs brokers
- materials engineers
- consultants
- researchers building open data tools

## Product modules

1. Situation Room
2. Upload & Analyze
3. Supplier Risk AI
4. Company Dictionary
5. Scenario Lab
6. Material Substitution Advisor
7. Data Sources & Deploy

## Recommended positioning

Do not position it as “CBAM calculator”. Position it as:

> Open-source CBAM intelligence and exposure radar for materials/manufacturing supply chains.
